Hollywood's best movies during 2022-2024 according to Siedah

Top 3 best movies of 20222-2024

1. Mean Girls, 20th anniversary.

If you're looking vibrancy, singing and an escape from reality, then this is our pick!

2. The Little Mermaid.

Omg! What can I say about this movie? It has everything, so much color and music. Halle Bailey was amazing. Her singing was absolutely beautiful. The entire crew did a good job with staying true to the original, while making the film very relevant to modern times.

Avatar: The Way of Water (2022).

Once you get past the length (seriously 3 hrs and 12 minutes), the visuals are stunning. Many people have implied that it's an reinactment of the 1st movie but wow, the creativity and vision behind this film deserve a special shout out.
